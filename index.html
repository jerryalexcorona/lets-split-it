Welcome to Let's Split It!

I created this tool to help me split my paycheck into several different accounts. Before spending any money, I enter the amount I received. Then, I specify how many accounts to divide it into. Here are the accounts I split it into: rent, travel, taxes, investments, emergencies, donations, savings, and my spending money. If I need to save for something new, like a phone or laptop, I can easily add another split.

I know this might seem a bit extreme for some, but you can start with two and increase the splits as needed.

Planning a vacation? Need a new car? Saving for a house down payment? Trying to pay off credit card debt? Whatever your goal, simply add another split and allocate money towards it.

I made it simple so you can split the total by fixed dollar amounts or percentages. For example, let's say every paycheck I allocate $600 to my first split, which I'll call rent. Then, I set aside $200 in my spending account for daily expenses until the next payday. My third split, named savings, receives 50%, and my fourth split, travel, gets 40%. The remaining amount goes into my final split for donations.
